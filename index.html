About Us

Our Story

A Legacy of Quality

Based in Vyasarpadi, Chennai, Royal Proteins & Foods is a family-run business dedicated to providing the finest quality mutton, chicken, and fresh fruits at fair prices. Every cut is carefully prepared to ensure freshness and hygiene.

Beyond retail meats, we offer event services including Shamiyana tent rental, decorations, cooking vessels, and professional cleaners — making us a one-stop shop for your gatherings and daily needs.
